ABOUT HIMTI COMPETITION

Hosted by HIMTI BINUS University, the HIMTI Competition is an exciting event that brings together the brightest talents in technology and design. Participants can showcase their skills in three dynamic fields: UI/UX Design, Competitive Programming, and App Creation. This competition is a platform for innovation, creativity, and excellence, offering participants the opportunity to solve real-world problems, create stunning user experiences, and develop innovative applications.

Join us and be a part of this prestigious event, where the future of technology is shaped!
